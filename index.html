Todco & Whiting Experts
These are the two most common roll-up door systems on commercial trucks in BC. We know them inside out — parts, tolerances, failure points, and the shortcuts that cause problems down the line.
TODCO
Todco Doors
Todco roll-up doors are industry-standard on delivery trucks and straight trucks across North America. We carry Todco-compatible springs, hinges, rollers, and panels, and understand the specific tension and track geometry these systems require.
WHIT
Whiting Doors
Whiting systems are common on refrigerated and cargo trucks operating in the Lower Mainland. Their spring and counterbalance systems are precise — improper adjustment causes premature wear. We set them correctly every time.
